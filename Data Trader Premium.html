All features are available inside our Discord server, which you’ll gain access right after purchasing

I’ve already purchased the membership, how do I access the features?

After purchase, you’ll be redirected to our Whop Hub to connect your Discord account. Once connected, you’ll receive "the Students" role to unlock all features inside the Discord server.

You can also access your membership through the confirmation email sent to your registered address.

If you encounter any issues after purchasing, chat with our support team through Whop, or open a support ticket in our Discord server: https://discord.gg/datatrader

Is this beginner-friendly?

Yes. Our Setups are clear and easy to follow even if you’re new to trading. You’ll also have direct access to pro analysts for help, plus guides to show you how to act on the signals properly.

How often do you send trade alerts?

On average, each of our analysts share alerts every 1–2 days depending on market conditions.

What markets do you cover?

We cover crypto (Bitcoin & Altcoins), forex (Currencies & Gold), and U.S.-listed stocks. Our setups include short-term day trades (a few hours to days) and long-term swing trades (weeks to months).

Can I ask questions or request charts?

Yes. As a Premium member, you’ll have direct access to our expert analysts, tag them anytime for chart reviews, feedback, or guidance.

Do you guarantee profitability?

No trading service can guarantee profitability, and past performance does not indicate future results. What we do guarantee is quality trade alerts and expert guidance designed to give you an edge and help you grow as a trader.
